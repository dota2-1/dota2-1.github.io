May 15 Dota 2 Update: Exchange Rates
On the night of May 15, developers released an update for the Dota 2 client.
List of changes:
Adjusted rates of a number of currencies. Items are now cheaper when buying for Canadian dollars, Indonesian rupees, Colombian and Chilean pesos and Kazakhstani tenge, but more expensive - for Brazilian realities and Argentinean pesos..
